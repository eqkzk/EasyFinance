User Guide

Welcome to EasyFinance! This platform helps manage your stock investments efficiently.
EasyFinance is an easy-to-use interface for tracking stock investments and making informed financial decisions.

After logging in, follow the on-screen prompts to navigate through the options.

  • Add Stock to Portfolio - Enter a stock ticker and number of shares.
  • Remove Stock - Specify a stock ticker and number of shares.
  • View Portfolio - Displays current stock holdings.
  • View Transactions - Access historical stock transactions.
  • Fetch Stock Info - Enter a stock ticker to retrieve stock details.
  • Update Risk Tolerance - Adjust your stock recommendations.
  • Import Portfolio - Upload a CSV file for easier access.

Welcome to EasyFinance

Helping you manage your stock investments efficiently.

  • Add Stock to Portfolio: Enter a stock ticker and the number of shares to purchase.
  • Remove Stock from Portfolio: Specify a stock ticker and the number of shares to remove.
  • View Portfolio: Displays your current stock holdings, including quantities, purchase prices,live price and realized profit/loss. As well as a pie chart for portfolio allocation.
  • View Past Transactions: Access to historical stock transactions, including purchases, sales, realised profit/loss and unrealised profit/loss.
  • Fetch Stock Info: Enter a stock ticker to retrieve details such as book value, market cap, trailing PE and forward PE. View the stock prices for the last 6 months to observe any trend.
  • Update Risk Tolerance: View your current risk tolerance and modify it to adjust the risk threshold for stock recommendations.
  • Import Portfolio: Import a CSV file if you have a existing portfolio for easier access.
